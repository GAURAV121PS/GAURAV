Oil and Textiles: Two Pillars of Global Industry Oil and textiles are two of the most significant industries shaping economies worldwide. While oil fuels transportation, energy, and manufacturing, textiles provide essential fabrics for clothing, home furnishings, and industrial applications. Oil Industry The oil industry plays a crucial role in modern civilization. Crude oil is extracted from the earth and refined into fuels like gasoline, diesel, and jet fuel. Additionally, petroleum-based products such as plastics, lubricants, and chemicals are widely used in daily life. The industry involves exploration, drilling, refining, and distribution, contributing significantly to global trade and employment.
